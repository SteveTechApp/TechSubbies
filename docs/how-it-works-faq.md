# TechSubbies How It Works FAQ

## Platform position

TechSubbies is a skills and availability matching service.

It helps customers discover independent AV and IT contractors by:

- skills
- role profile
- location
- availability
- specialist experience
- visible profile information

TechSubbies does not:

- recruit contractors
- employ contractors
- supply workers
- manage site delivery
- negotiate rates
- collect payment for work
- pay engineers
- guarantee income
- guarantee that a customer will proceed

## Commercial arrangement

Any commercial arrangement is made directly between the customer and the independent contractor.

This includes:

- scope
- rate
- terms
- payment
- tax
- insurance
- site attendance
- delivery responsibility
- dispute handling

## Opportunity Visibility Guarantee

If an eligible paying engineer member receives no suitable opportunity alerts during a paid subscription month, TechSubbies adds one additional month to their subscription at no extra cost.

The guarantee does not apply where a suitable opportunity was sent and the member:

- declined it
- ignored it
- let it expire
- countered unsuccessfully
- changed availability after receipt
- chose not to proceed with the customer

The guarantee also does not apply where the member's incomplete profile, unavailable calendar, narrow preferences or missing information prevented suitable matching.

## Suitable opportunity alert

A suitable alert should match:

- active specialist profile
- selected role category
- required skills
- location or travel radius
- stated availability
- work preferences
- visible opportunity details
- any required document/compliance status

## Communication log

The platform should log:

- opportunity ID
- customer ID
- contractor ID
- match reason
- sent time
- viewed time
- response status
- messages
- expired or withdrawn status
- guarantee eligibility decision
