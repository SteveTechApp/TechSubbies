# TechSubbies Company Engineer Management Dashboard

The company dashboard gives resourcing companies, subcontracting businesses, AV/IT service providers and installation firms a central place to manage engineers.

## Supports

- Central engineer management
- Company-wide profile updates
- Group-based updates
- Individual engineer drill-down
- Compliance document tracking
- Audit history
- Engineer ownership separation

## Ownership models

| Model | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| engineer_owned | Engineer owns the profile and can be invited into company pools |
| company_managed | Company creates and maintains the record |
| linked_shared | Engineer owns core profile, company owns company-specific overlays |

## Product rule

Company accounts should manage company-owned information without taking control away from independent engineers.
